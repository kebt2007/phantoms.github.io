A Mind-Bending Experience.

The Museum of Illusions


We are Phantoms Design Team, a newfound architecture group founded in March 2022. Based in Charlotte, North Carolina, we look to work on local buildings and renovate, make, and design current or new architecture projects. Our first one is the PLTW Library Keystone 2.0 EXPO Project, where we are designing a library museum with an aspirational or historical theme. Our chosen theme ended up being illusions and our team set out to create an amazing attraction for the current generation while paying homage to the history of this magical element.
